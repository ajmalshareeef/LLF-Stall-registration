What is LLF?

Litera Literature Fest (LLF) is a two-day event happening on February 24 and 25 at SAFI College. Organized by the SAFI Readers' Forum in association with the RAFTHA Students' Union, the festival celebrates literature and creativity.

With thought-provoking sessions, hands-on workshops, and lively panel discussions, LLF is set to inspire writers and readers alike. Join us for this unforgettable celebration!
