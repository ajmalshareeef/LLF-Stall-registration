What is LLF?

Litera Literature Fest (LLF) is an exciting two-day event happening on February 24 and 25 at SAFI College. Organized by the SAFI Readers' Forum in association with the RAFTHA Students' Union, the festival celebrates literature and creativity.

Attendees can look forward to thought-provoking sessions, hands-on workshops, lively panel discussions, and the presence of notable guests ready to inspire budding writers and avid readers.

Join us for this unforgettable celebration of literature, uniting book lovers from all walks of life!
